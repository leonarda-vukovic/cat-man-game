Game Rules

Objective

Your goal is to survive all levels and rounds without losing all your lives.

If you lose all your lives, the cat catches you.

How the Game Works

The game is divided into 4 difficulty levels: Easy, Medium, Hard, and Extra Hard.

Each level contains 10 rounds.
In every round, you must guess a hidden word or phrase within a limited number of guesses.

Difficulty Levels

As you progress, the game will progressively increase in difficulty according to several factors:

  • The length of the word or phrase
  • The complexity of vocabulary or terminology
  • The number of incorrect guesses allowed

Level specifications:

  • Easy: 10 lives, 6 guesses per round
  • Medium: 8 lives, 6 guesses per round
  • Hard: 6 lives, 5 guesses per round
  • Extra Hard: 3 lives, 5 guesses per round

Lives

You start with a set number of lives depending on the chosen level.

Each time you lose a round, you lose one life and the cat gets closer.

When you finish a level, you automatically move to the next one.
Your remaining lives carry over - they do not reset.

Losing and Progression

If you fail a round, you still move on to the next one.
Rounds cannot be replayed during the same game session.

If you lose all your lives, the game ends, meaning the cat got you.

Guessing

You can:

  • Guess letters one by one
  • Or guess the entire word/phrase at once

A wrong full-word guess counts the same as a wrong letter - it uses up a guess and can cost you a life.

Special Buttons

You have limited help available:

  • Hints (3 total): Gives a clue about the word or phrase and it does not cost a life.
  • Skip (1 total): Skips the current round without losing a life.

Categories

Each word belongs to one of three categories:

  • Food
  • Countries
  • Animals

The category will be displayed at the start of every round.

Final Goal

Reach the final round of the Extra Hard level and survive.

That's the only way to escape the cat.
